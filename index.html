Fun Guy

"Fun Guy" is the nickname of the iögo nanö yogurt drinks. Fun Guy believes misery = fun, sad = happy,
boredom = exitement, and hell = heaven. He will always try to make his food friends have fun by doing
group activities such as jumping of cliffs, doing nothing, jumping into human mouths, bleeding, etc.

Fun Guy is believed to be the main supporter of the killer bunny invasion, and the super spy water bottle
guys recently found out that Fun Guy has a cloning machine in his basement. Since the cloning machine
runs on apple juice, the Fun Guys must be getting apple juice from somewhere. The water bottle guys
think that there is a new secret apple Fun Guy that is powering the machine.
